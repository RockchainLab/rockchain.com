RHT Rockchain Advisory.

RHT and Rockchain have entered into a strategic joint venture focused on the Asian Token Economy - RHT Rockchain Advisory Pte. Ltd, based in Singapore.

Our goal is to provide an holistic and integrated set of services that combines the strength of RHT Holdings, Asia's leading professional services group with Rockchain, a specialist blockchain technology and advisory firm and one of only a small number of Approved Sponsor Firms of the Gibraltar Blockchain Exchange (GBX).

Together, we are supporting blockchain projects, Token Sales (Initial Coin Offerings or ICOs) and emerging Security Token Offerings (STOs). We also develop a range of innovative products and solutions delivering value and synergies to its clients - investors and innovators alike.
